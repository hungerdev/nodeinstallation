School Management System

e-School automates all required tasks from student admission to pass out, maintain academic records, attendance, fee, examination, teachers bio data, attendance and payroll. It provide a lot of reporting for printing and analysis.School Management System is a software that automates the life cycle of a student from admission to pass-out, maintains academic records, facilitates collaboration, allows schools to collect fees, conduct exams and print report cards. eSchool has modules to manage Timetable, Attendance, Examinations, mark sheets,Library,Events and many more. eSchool has a accounts module to manage the payroll and employee pay slips. The Finance module helps you to plan and assign different fee structures to students. eSchool has an internal messaging system through this we can interact with parents.
